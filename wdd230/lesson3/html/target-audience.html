Warm or Cold Weather

Target Audience

  • Home
  • Target Audience
  • Color Scheme
  • Typography
  • Who: Residents and visitors of Idaho
  • Age: 12-80 years old
  • Income: All income levels
  • Motivation: To check the weather in order to plan for activities

Persona 1

Payson Henderson is a stay at home mom. She is a homemaker, a wife and a mother of 2 boys. She is 30 years old and is currently working on her nursing degree. She loves to spend time enjoying outdoors. Her goals and tasks are taking care of her home, going to school and caring for her family. She divides her time between home and school.

Scenarios

  • She wants to take her boys to the park and wants to know if it will snow
  • Her family is wanting to drive from Colorado and needs to know if it will be sunny
  • She wants to go camping and needs to know if the weather will be nice
  • She is getting her house painted and needs to know if it will rain
  • She is leaving school late and wants to make sure it's not snowing

Persona 2

Bob Smith is a 21 year old student visiting from BYU Provo. Bob is single and loves to date. He is majoring in psycology. He has a sister who he is visiting and she has 4 daughters. His reason for visiting is because his sister is setting him up on a blind date. He is hoping this is his last first date.

Scenarios

  • He needs to know if it is snowing because he is taking his blind date skiing
  • He is driving home to Utah and would like to know if it is going to be snowing.
  • He wants to take his nieces ice skating and wants to make sure it is cold enough
  • He wants to go running in the morning of his date and would like to know if it will be foggy
  • Wants to drive to walmart and needs to make sure its not snowing
